About us

S.K Steel is a commercial kitchen equipment manufacturer and fabricator specializing in producing high-quality products for foodservice operations of all sizes. Our extensive product line includes everything from cooking, bakery, display counter, and refrigeration equipment to Cold Rooms and Exhaust & Ducting systems. In addition to our wide range of standard products.

Our company specializes in the production of kitchen equipment, suitable for large hotels, fast food joints, restaurants, bars, supermarkets, snack bars, hostel, hospital, canteen, and other places, with more than 200 varieties and specifications, to meet the different needs of various customers. We at S.K Steel Kitchen Equipment follow all International standards keeping in mind the safety of users.
